Robot Moderation Commands
  • about - Posts a small embed explaining what Robot Moderation is.
  • announce - Posts a small embed with whatever text you want.
  • avatar - Posts your avatar link.
  • ban - Bans a mentioned user, with a reason.
  • help - Posts a link to this website.
  • invite - Posts the invite link to add Robot Moderation to your server.
  • kick - Kicks a mentioned user, with a reason.
  • ping - Pings your connection to the bot with a milliseconds value.
  • purge - Purges X amount of messages in a given channel.
  • reload - Reloads a given command.
  • setup - Sets you up with a Staff role.
